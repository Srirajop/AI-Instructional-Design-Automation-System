**PROJECT INFORMATION**

*   **Project Name:** Cyber Security For Employees
*   **Business Unit:** IT
*   **Target Audience:** All Employees
*   **Experience Level:** 0-2 years
*   **Date:** 2026-02-21

**COURSE OVERVIEW**

*   **Context/Background:** Cyber threats and attacks are increasingly sophisticated, affecting individuals, small businesses, and large organizations. To address these threats, it is crucial for employees to understand cyber security fundamentals and learn how to handle malware attacks effectively. This training aims to equip employees with the necessary knowledge and skills to protect the organization's digital assets.
*   **Project Goal:** By the end of this training, employees will be able to identify and handle malware attacks, understand the importance of cyber security, and recognize early warning signs of cyber threats.
*   **Duration:** Estimated 6 hours

**LEARNING OBJECTIVES**

*   Learners will be able to handle malware attacks happening in organization.
*   Learners will be able to identify and understand the types of malware and cyber attacks encountered in day-to-day work.
*   Learners will be able to recognize early warning signs of cyber threats and understand the impact of security breaches on business continuity.
*   Learners will be able to explain why cyber security is critical for organizations and understand the importance of data encryption, integrity, and availability.
